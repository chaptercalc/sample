Why It Works (Movements / Motivation)

MOVEMENTS: We needed our program to address TWO issues. First, to select the best movements (stretches) which will achieve finest results. Second, arrange those stretches according to ability (beginner, intermediate, expert) in a comprehensive guide for our user’s success.

MOTIVATION: The best programing on the planet isn’t going to work if your members don’t stay motivated. This is why we have you pledge a dollar amount that you earn back! That’s right, you have to earn back your own money by completing the program.
